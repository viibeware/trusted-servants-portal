Visibility

Off-switch for Recovery Contacts site-wide. When off, /contactlist returns 404, the sidebar section disappears, and any nav link pointed at the form falls back to its URL. Existing entries and the settings below are preserved when off.
  • Recovery Contacts enabled

    Hard off-switch. Entries and settings are preserved when off.

    • Who can review & approve entries is set on Settings → Modules (the Recovery Contacts access role).

    Admin email alerts

    New submissions always appear as a chip in the Recovery Contacts admin panel. These toggles decide whether you also get an email — both off by default.
  • Email me about new submissions

    Covers new entries and update requests. The admin-panel chip always shows regardless of this setting.

  • Email me about removal requests

    Sent only after the person clicks the confirmation link we email them — so you're never asked to remove someone who hasn't confirmed they want off the list. The pending request still appears in the admin panel right away.

    • Comma-separated for multiple recipients. Falls back to your access-request recipients ({{ site.access_request_to or '(not set)' }}) when blank.

    Page copy

    Heading, subheading, and intro shown above the directory + form. Leave a field blank to use its built-in default.

    Confirmation

    Shown to the visitor after a successful submission.

    Layout

    Container width for the public page. Boxed caps the content at a max width and centers it; Full width spans the viewport with side padding.

    Bot protection

    A hidden honeypot field traps simple form-fillers automatically. Cloudflare Turnstile rides on the existing site-wide setting — when it's on, it's on for this form too.
